US Territory Shipping Options

This form may be used to determine the appropriate shipping method and cost for an order being shipped to a US Territory.

Orders over $100 receive free shipping. The shipping method is 'USPS Priority Flat Rate'. This order may be quoted for expedited shipping if requested (expedited orders will ship via UPS). NOTE: Orders being expedited to US Territories often do not arrive on the expected date. We do not suggest attempting to expedite these orders and will not refund the cost if the order does not arrive "on time". The customer should be informed of this if they request expedited shipping.
